Course Description

Welcome to the comprehensive course on ""Sensitive and Damaged Skin Care.""! This course covers two major topics: sensitive skin and damaged skin.

Sensitive Skin:

   > We start by delving into sensitive skin. If you think sensitive skin is just one type, you're mistaken. "Sensitive skin" is a broad term that includes various conditions. In the initial part of our diploma, we touched on some types of sensitive skin, such as eczema-prone sensitive skin. In this course, we will dive deeper into other types of sensitive skin, such as rosacea-prone and seborrheic dermatitis-prone skin, among others. Each type of sensitive skin will be examined using renowned global cosmetic brands like La Roche-Posay, Bioderma, Isispharma, and Uriage.

Damaged Skin:

   > In the second part of the course, we will shift our focus to damaged skin. We will explore the causes of skin damage and the active ingredients essential for its treatment. We will apply our knowledge to analyze and understand the formulations and uses of products from leading cosmetic brands. By the end of this course, you will have a thorough understanding of various cosmetic products designed for both sensitive and damaged skin.

This course emphasizes a deep understanding of many global cosmetic brands in terms of sensitive and damaged skin care. At the end of each section, a quick summary will ensure you are well-acquainted with all the products discussed.
