Course Description

Unlock the secrets behind staying fresh and confident with "The Complete Guide to Deodorant & Antiperspirant." This comprehensive course dives deep into the science, effectiveness, and practical use of sweat control solutions.

Throughout this course, you will explore the biological foundation of sweat and its essential role in the human body. You'll gain a thorough understanding of how deodorants and antiperspirants work, the active ingredients that make them effective, and the best practices for their application.

We'll cover various types of hyperhidrosis and provide scientifically grounded methods to manage each condition. You'll learn to evaluate deodorant and antiperspirant products from a scientific perspective, ensuring you can make informed decisions based on solid evidence.

By analyzing collections from well-known brands like Vichy and Uriage, we'll highlight key differences, strengths, and uses of their products. We'll also delve into budget-friendly options from brands like Nivea, offering a practical guide to choosing effective and affordable

Additionally, we'll address important topics such as the correct way to apply these products, debunking myths about their potential health risks, and understanding the relationship between aluminum salts in antiperspirants and breast cancer.

By the end of this course, you'll become an expert in managing any sweating condition, recommending the most suitable products, and scientifically evaluating any sweat-related products from companies worldwide.
